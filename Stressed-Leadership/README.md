# Stressed Leadership: The 3D Human Layer
In an industry obsessed with digital "Velocity," we've lost the Tactile Signal. This repository houses the models, concepts, and philosophy for humanizing the tech stack through Physical Artifacts and Peer Bonding.

##  The Philosophy: Beyond the Screen
We are debugging the "Stressed Leader" stereotype by bringing the invisible layers of tech leadership into the physical world.

3D Printing as Culture: We don't just "deploy" code; we "print" identity. These figures (The Tech Wrangler, CISO) serve as physical totems for team alignment.

Peer Bonding as Infrastructure: If the "Human Nodes" aren't connected by trust, the "Server Nodes" will eventually fail. We are optimizing for Relational Throughput.

## Why GitHub for 3D Models?
Because Bonding is Open Source.

- Tactile Reliability: You can't "delete" a physical object. It stays on the desk during an all hands on outage.
- Shared Ownership: When a team "prints" their culture together, they own the Source Code of their success.
- Humanizing the Node: We are moving from "Stressed Stereotypes" to High-Fidelity Professionals.
